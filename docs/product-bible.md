# Product Bible - Elfirma Platform

## Vision

A complete operational platform for small Swedish electrical companies, covering the workflow from first customer contact to completed invoice basis.

## Target company

Reference company:

Ljungqvist Elservice AB
Size: 6 people
Team:
- 1 owner/manager
- 1 part-time administrator
- 3 electricians
- 1 apprentice

Market:
- Private customers
- Small businesses
- BRFs
- Property owners

Common jobs:
- Fault tracing
- Service jobs
- EV charger installation
- Electrical panel replacement
- Lighting installation
- Kitchen renovation electrical work
- Small commercial installations
- Emergency jobs

Current tools:
- Phone
- SMS
- Email
- Calendar
- Excel
- Fortnox
- Grossist apps
- Personal notes

Main problems:
- Jobs are forgotten
- Work orders are unclear
- Time is reported late
- Materials are missed on invoices
- Documentation is spread across phones
- Invoices are sent too late
- The owner has too much in their head

## Core workflow

1. Customer contacts company
2. Admin or manager registers customer/request
3. Decision: direct booking, site visit, quote, or reject
4. Work order is created
5. Work order is assigned to electrician
6. Electrician sees job in mobile view
7. Electrician performs work
8. Electrician reports time, material, notes, and photos
9. Work order is marked complete
10. Admin/manager reviews
11. Invoice draft is generated
12. Invoice is created in external accounting system later

## Product promise

Less admin.
Faster invoicing.
Fewer missed materials.
Better control.
Simpler day for electricians.
